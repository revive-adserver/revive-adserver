By default OpenX Market will serve ads in your zones if no other ads are to be served. You can change this behavior for any zone by editing that zone.

What is OpenX Market?

OpenX Market is a service that helps place ads on your sites to make you more money. It uses a real-time auction of hundreds of ad buyers to get the highest paying ad for each of your ad impressions.
